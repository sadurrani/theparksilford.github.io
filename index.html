About Us

The Parks (Ilford) Management Company is a Residentsâ€™ Management Company (RMC) that has been formed to protect the interests of leaseholders of a residential building. The RMC is responsible for the management and maintenance of the buildings/estate, as well as compliance with the obligations of the lease and a range of statutory requirements (subject to the lease structure). The directors of the estate, who are unpaid and appointed by the members, decides how best to achieve this by appointing a professional managing agent.


Managing Agent

The managing agent is a company that is appointed by the directors to manage the estate. They manage lease and transfers, contracts and general upkeep and repair of the estate. On the direction of the directors, they arrange insurance, contracts for cleaning and ground maitenance and other day to day activities for repairs.

The Parks (Ilford) is a private residential estate. This means council do not pick up the bulk refusal items like Fridge, Furniture, Sofa and Beds. How to arrange Bulk Waste:

Redbridge council allows residents to arrange the large item collection for free. Please follow these steps:
  1. Book the collection with the council.
  2. A night before the collection date, place the item outside the house or near bin area (if live in flats)
  3. Make sure proper notices are affixed on the bulk waste items. Affix the council collection confirmation as well as your address so everyone is aware that it will be collected by council.
